The data set has power consumption data(kw) for 30 houses(labelled with house numbers) from 01 Jan 2019 to 01 Nov 2019 at a 15 minute sampling rate.
Each column has data for a specific data point(house).
15 minute data sampling is considered enough to capture patterns in energy consumption across different houses and for a specific house.
size of file - 6048kb
Source of data - Pecan Street Dataport(login required) - https://dataport.pecanstreet.org/
How accurate is the date? - Data from meters placed at each unit. No abstractions in data.
Note - S0me houses do not have data for all intervals
